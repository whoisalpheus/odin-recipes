Dark n Stormy Recipe

Easy Ingredient Dark n Stormy

The Dark 'n Stormy is a delicious, easy-to-make cocktail that features a refreshing trio of dark rum, ginger beer and lime.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. dark rum (Kraken is the best, imo
  • 1/2 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 3-4 oz. ginger beer (1/2 a can basically)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass(preferably highball) with ice.
  2. Add lime juice.
  3. Add the ginger beer over the ice.
  4. Pour dark rum over ginger beer.
  5. Garnish with lime wedge or wheel.
