Margarita Recipe

Easy Ingredient Margarita

The Margarita is a classic for a reason! Just three ingredientsâ€”tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice. Salted rim optional.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz. blanco tequila
  • 1 oz. orange liqueur
  • 1/2 oz. fresh lime juice
  • Optional ice and/or kosher salt

Steps

  1. Salt the rim of your glass(optional) by rubbing with lime wedge and dipping into salt.
  2. Add ice to glass. (optional)
  3. Pour tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice in shaker filled with ice.
  4. Shake for 10-15 seconds and strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with lime wedge.
